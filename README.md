## DATA
### CrackSeg9k dataset
CrackSeg9k consists of 10 public cracking data sets: Deep Crack, Crack 500, Crack Tree 200, CFD, Ceramic, Masonry, Volcker, SDNET 2018, Risbilder, and GAPS 384, covering roads, buildings and other scenes.
The download link is: https://github.com/Dhananjay42/crackseg9k

### EdmCrack600 dataset
EdmCrack600 was collected at an oblique angle using a high-speed motion camera mounted at the rear of a vehicle,covering diverse road conditions such as shadows, rainy weather, pavement textures, and varying illumination. It contains 600 pavement crack images (1920×1080) with pixel-level annotations(480 training and 120 testing samples).
Its download link is：https://github.com/mqp2259/EdmCrack600
